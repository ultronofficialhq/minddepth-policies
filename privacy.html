Privacy Policy — MindDepth

Last updated: 2026-06-14

MindDepth ("we", "us") operates an automated content publishing tool for posting videos to social media platforms, including TikTok, on behalf of its own accounts.

1. Information We Collect

This tool does not collect personal information from end users. It only uses authentication tokens (provided via OAuth) to publish content to MindDepth's own connected social media accounts.

2. How We Use Information

Authentication tokens are used exclusively to upload and manage video content on MindDepth's own accounts. We do not access, store, or share data belonging to other users or third parties.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally and securely, and are not shared with any third party.

4. Third-Party Platforms

This tool interacts with TikTok's Content Posting API solely to publish content. Use of TikTok is also subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

5. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: support.fhapp@gmail.com
